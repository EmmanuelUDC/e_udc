Community Mission and Vision

To educate and develop community members (including students and lecturers) of the University of Nigeria, Nsukka with basic knowledge of Cryptocurrency on Blockchain Education and development.

We have the vision of being the best Campus Blockchain Community in Africa.

Key skills we educate persons on;
  • Community Management/Business Management
  • Financial Market Analysis/Crypto Trading
  • Content Creation and Writing
  • Web Development
  • Blockchain Development
  • Web2
  • Web3
  • Arts/NFT/Graphics Designing
  • Decentralized Finance (DEFI)
  • Social Media Management
  • Blockchain Education
